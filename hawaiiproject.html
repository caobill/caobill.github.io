A Vision for Hawaii

Document at the bottom of the page- might take a second to load!

For a City Planning 110, I was tasked to think about how a city might look 50 years from now, and write a piece on it. However, while researching, I got really enthusiastic about the unique situation that Hawaii faces, and after the assignment was due, I spent some time to put together a more polished document on it.

However, I want to reiterate that I am NOT native Hawaiian, so please take my analysis with a grain of salt. I could never describe the native Hawaiian experience, and therefore I don't think I could ever imagine a system that works for them as well as they could for themselves. That being said, hope you find my analysis interesting!
